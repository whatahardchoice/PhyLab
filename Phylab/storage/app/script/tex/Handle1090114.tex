${\Delta}d_i$的A类误差：$$u_a({{\Delta}d_i})=\sqrt{\displaystyle\frac{\sum\limits_{i=1}^{5} ({{\Delta}d_i}_i-\bar{{\Delta}d_i})^2}{5{\times}(5-1)}}=%% UA_DELTA %% mm$$
